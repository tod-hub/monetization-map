🔍 Questions to Ask the Team

These are the input-level questions that reveal whether execution is improving or we're doing the same thing expecting different results.

● Content → Capture

"Are we getting people OFF platforms and INTO our funnel?"
  1. What is the CTA on every piece of content published this quarter? Is it consistent or random?
  2. Where does YouTube specifically send people — email list, DM keyword, or just "subscribe"?
  3. What % of content has a direct handraiser CTA (COACH/ELITE) vs lead magnet (BONUS/SWITCH) vs no CTA?
  4. IG Stories handraisers run Sun/Wed — what's the actual conversion rate? Are we running them consistently?
  5. LinkedIn generates 30+ leads/week — through what mechanism? Manual DM or content CTAs?

● Capture → Qualify

"Once someone raises their hand, what actually happens?"
  1. What are our email open rates and click rates on nurture sequences? Trending up or down?
  2. What's the DM-to-booked-call conversion rate? How many of 30,761 chats became calls?
  3. How many leads fall out between "keyword DM" and "SDR pickup"? What's the drop-off?
  4. SMS delivery/response rates — are messages even landing? (SMS/Web collapsed 81%)
  5. How many leads sit in nurture longer than 21 days with no outcome?

● Qualify → Close

"Lead quality is equal. Distribution is not. Chris gets 51% of all leads."
  1. Chris gets 51% of leads and produces the most — but is his close RATE higher or is it just volume? What's his % vs everyone else on equal footing?
  2. What does Adam do differently to hit 1.32% with fewer leads? Has anyone shadowed him?
  3. Why does Chris get 51%? Tenure? Round-robin weighting? If we equalized distribution, would total revenue go up or down?
  4. What's the no-show rate on booked calls? Is that changing?
  5. How many touches to close? Is that number going up (harder sells)?

● Close: Pricing & Mix

"Not discounting. Dan AI ($2K/yr, $1.2K/6mo) is pulling avg deal size down by design."
  1. What % of Mar deals are Dan AI ($2K) vs Elite ($9K)? If Dan AI volume is up, that explains the deal size drop — it's a feature, not a bug.
  2. Dan AI is offered to unqualified leads + existing members — is it cannibalizing Elite upgrades? Are people choosing $2K instead of $9K?
  3. What's the close rate by product? If Dan AI closes at 3%+ while Elite is at 0.2%, the strategy is working — just need more volume.
  4. What's the LTV path? How many Dan AI members upgrade to Elite within 12 months? If it's a real front door, the lower deal size is an investment.

● The Execution Question

"Is the machine doing more, or just the same?"
  1. How many pieces of content published per week — Jan vs Feb vs Mar? (volume)
  2. How many CTAs activated per week? (conversion intent)
  3. How many DMs sent by automation per week? (capture volume)
  4. How many calls booked per week? (pipeline generation)
  5. How many calls completed per week? (pipeline execution)

🔴 What's Broken — Fix These to Unlock Growth

1. Close Rate in Free Fall: 0.87% → 0.32% Through Q1

Close rate dropped every month this quarter. At 1% target with current chat volume (30K+/mo), that's 308 sales vs 99 today. Revenue upside: $1.25M/month. Three closers significantly underperform while Adam proves 1.32% is achievable.

→ Fix: Clone Adam's process. Audit SDR handoff quality. Determine if leads are bad (qualification issue) or closers need training (sales issue). Each 0.1% = $125K/mo.

2. SMS/Web Channel Collapsed 81%: $442K → $84K

Was the #1 revenue channel in January. Something broke — platform change? CTA conflict? Automation failure? Due to 2-month lag correlation (r=0.42), this collapse will hit revenue hardest in May-June if not reversed now.

→ Fix: URGENT. Reverse-engineer what changed between Jan and Feb. Check SMS delivery rates, website funnel, GoHighLevel automation. This is the single biggest revenue leak.

3. Deal Size Drop: $7K → $6K — Likely Dan AI Mix Shift (Not Discounting)

No unauthorized discounting. Dan AI was introduced at $2K/yr ($1.2K/6mo) for unqualified leads and existing members. This naturally pulls avg deal size down. The real question: is Dan AI a front door that feeds Elite upgrades, or is it cannibalizing the $9K product?

→ Investigate: Track Dan AI → Elite upgrade rate. If it's a real funnel (Dan AI → Elite → Quantum), the lower deal size is an investment in LTV. If members are staying at $2K forever, it's revenue dilution.

4. Email Revenue Down 57%: No Clear Recovery Path

Weak correlation (r=0.34) suggests email may not be a reliable channel. Slight Mar recovery but still way below Jan baseline. Check if this is a deliverability issue or fundamental channel problem.

→ Fix: Audit email deliverability, open rates, and CTR. If fundamentally broken, reallocate resources to Instagram (r=0.58, proven) and LinkedIn (r=0.58, growing).

5. Alumni Program Not Built — $660K/yr Left on Table

~1,100 Elite non-renewals per year with no downsell path. A $2K alumni tier at 30% conversion = $660K annual revenue recovered from churned customers.

→ Fix: Build and launch the alumni tier. Low-lift, high-impact. Captures revenue from customers who've already been sold once.

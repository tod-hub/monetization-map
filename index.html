🔴 Critical Problems

1. Close Rate Catastrophe: 0.32% vs 1.0% Target

Only 99 sales from 30,761 chats. If we hit 1% target, that's 308 sales = +$1.25M/month additional revenue. Three closers (Chris 0.30%, Jay 0.25%, Nolan 0.18%) severely underperform while Adam hits 1.32%.

→ Fix: Audit SDR→Closer handoff. Is qualification broken (bad leads) or closers need training? Clone Adam's process.

2. Deal Size Erosion: $7K → $6K Avg (-15% YTD)

Average deal size dropped from $7K (Jan, 320 deals) → $6K (Feb, 275 deals) → $5,979 (Mar MTD, 183 deals). Fewer deals AND smaller deals = double revenue compression.

→ Fix: Review sales process. Are closers discounting to hit targets? Are we attracting smaller clients? Enforce minimum deal size or raise prices.

3. Email Channel Collapse: $175K → $75K (-57%)

Email revenue fell off a cliff in Feb. Something broke in the lead capture → email nurture pipeline around late January. Weak correlation (r=0.34) suggests the channel itself may not be viable long-term.

→ Fix: Audit email automation. Check deliverability, open rates, CTA performance. If broken, kill it and reallocate budget to Instagram/LinkedIn (proven r=0.58 channels).

4. SMS/Web Channel Obliterated: $442K → $84K (-81%)

Website/SMS channel (strongest revenue correlation per prior analysis) collapsed from $442K (Jan, 58 deals) → $268K (Feb) → $84K (Mar MTD, ~20 deals projected). This breakdown will compound in May-June due to 2-month lag effect.

→ Fix: URGENT. Reverse-engineer what broke. Was it a platform change? CTA conflict? Automation failure? This is the single biggest revenue leak.
